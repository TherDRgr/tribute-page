ATTY. MA. LEONOR "LENI" G. ROBREDO

The 14th Vice President of the Philippines

A lawyer, a public servant, and a true epitome of leadership, she's one of the person that started Angat Buhay, a non-governmental organization, she started as a representative of Camarines Sur's third district in 2013, prior to entering politics, she was a lawyer that provides legal assistance to marginalized clients, and focuses on legal developmental work. She also led numerous relief opreations through the Office of the Vice President, the same office that managed a comprehensive COVID-19 response program that offers a range of assistance.
